INTERNATIONAL SALE OF GOODS AGREEMENT (EXCERPT)

This Agreement is made between Pacific Components Ltd. ("Buyer", a Vietnamese SME) and
Apex Global Trading GmbH ("Seller").

Article 1 — Payment
The Buyer shall pay 50% of the total contract value in advance upon signing, with the
remaining balance due within 7 days of the bill of lading date, by T/T to the Seller's account.

Article 2 — Penalty for Breach
If the Buyer delays acceptance or payment for any reason, the Buyer shall pay a penalty of
15% of the total contract value. The Seller shall bear no penalty for late delivery.

Article 3 — Governing Law and Disputes
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Germany. Any dispute shall be finally settled
by arbitration administered in Frankfurt, in the German language.

Article 4 — Inspection
Goods shall be deemed accepted unless the Buyer notifies defects within 3 days of arrival at
the destination port. The Seller's quality certificate shall be conclusive and binding.

Article 5 — Termination
The Seller may terminate this Agreement at any time with 7 days' notice. The Buyer may
terminate only upon a material breach by the Seller that remains uncured for 60 days.

Article 6 — Confidentiality
The Buyer shall not disclose any information relating to the Seller, its products, or this
Agreement to any third party, indefinitely, including after termination.
